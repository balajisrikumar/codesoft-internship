Language and literature

Language is a method of communication, while literature is the content being communicated. Language has been described as a set of gestures and words and phrases with meaning behind them, while literature is the manipulation and use of those gestures and words and phrases for creative purposes.

Music & Finearts

Music is considered fine art, which is also referred to as high art. It is a form of art that is focused on esthetics and is created to have a pleasing effect on the audience or onlookers. It requires a certain set of skills to create something that qualifies as fine art.
